Covid 19 virus is a SARS (Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome) virus which was spread from a animal market in Wuhan,China.
This virus affects our respiratory system by attacking our lungs and blocking the alveloi with mucus
The virus is contagious and it can spread through touch and water droplets entering your body through nose or mouth from the infected person
The virus is fatal and has caused over 6 lakhs fatalities worldwide
USA is the state with maximu number of cases
India stands on 3rd postion as far as number of cases are concerned with more than 15 lakhs cases
It has been declared as a global pandemic by WHO

Measures taken:

Many countries after seeing an increase in their corona virus cases imposed a lockdown to stop the virus spread
But due to this millions of people lost their jobs. Migrants in our country faced problems in going home.
Economies started shrinking. Though various governments released various relief packages but still the virus is causing gdp to shrink
US economy has shrinked by 33% - the worst ever GDP decline
Indian GDP is also expected to shrink by 5% or even more

After some months of lockdown , the procedure of unlocking started.
People were asked to follow the following norms
  • Don't go out of home unless necessary
  • Wear proper mask
  • Maintain a distance of 2 feet while talking
  • Avoid goint to publc gatherings
  • Wash you hands or use sanitizer as frequently as possible
